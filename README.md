# Hi, I'm Deepak Kushawaha 👋

I'm a Ph.D. Scholar in Economics at the **Indira Gandhi Institute of Development Research (IGIDR)**, Mumbai. My thesis studies the **financing dynamics of the renewable energy transition in developing economies**.

I share code, data, and replication packages here so other researchers can reproduce and build on the work. Each release is archived on **Zenodo** with a citable DOI.
